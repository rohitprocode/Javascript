Polymorphism in Javascript //OOP

Polymorphism merans 'many forms', and it occurs when we have many classes that are related to each other by inheritance.Like we specified in the previous chapter; Inheritance lets us inherit attributes and methods from another class.Polymorphism uses those methods to perform different tasks.

Example: 1

1. A person at the same time ca have different characteristics.Like a man at the same time is a father,a husband,an employee.So ther person posses different behaviour in different situations.This is the real life example of polymorphism.

2. Call centre is also the example of polymorphism.When you call at call centre you get that:-
Press 0 to call, Press 1 to cancel, Press 2 to order.So this process in which we get different output in the same function, this is called as polymorphism.
