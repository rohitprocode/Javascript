Short Date

Short Dates are written with an "MM/DD/YYYY" format.


In same browsers, months or days with no leading zeroes may produce an error.


The behaviour of "YYYY-MM-DD" is undefined.Some browsers will try to guess the format.Some will return NaN.


The behaviour of "DD-MM-YYYY" is also undefined.Some browsers will try to guess the format.Some will return NaN.
