ISO Dates

ISO 8601 is the international standard for the representationof dates and times. 

            Description: Year and Month Format: YYYY-MM Example:2018-06
            Description: Only Year      Format: YYYY    Example:2018
            Description: Date and Time  Format: YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM:SSZ Example: 2018-06-21T12:00:00Z
            Description: Date and Time  Format: YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM:SS+HH:MM Example: 2018-06-21T12:00:00+06:30
            Description: Date and Time  Format: YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM:SS-HH:MM Example: 2018-06-21T12:00:00-06:30

Date and Time is separated with capital T.


UTC time is defined with a capital letter Z


If you modify a time relative to UTC, remove the Z and add + HH:MM or -HH:MM instead.
