Airthmetic Operators

Meaning of operator "+" is Addition

Meaning of operator "-" is Subtraction

Meaning of operator "*" is Multiplication

Meaning of operator "/" is Division

Meaning of operator "%" is Modulus operator to get remainder in integer division.

Meaning of operator "++" is Increment

Meaning of operator "--" is Decrement

Relational/Comparison Operators

Meaning of operator "<" is Less than

Meaning of operator ">" is Greater than

Meaning of operator "<=" is Less than or equal to

Meaning of operator ">=" is Greater than or equal to

Meaning of operator "==" is Equal to

Meaning of operator "!=" is Not equal to

Meaning of operator "===" is Equal value and same type

Meaning of operator "!==" is Not equal value to or not same typr

Logical Operator

Meaning of operator "&&" is Logical and

Meaning of operator "||" is Logical or

Meaning of operator "!" is Logical not

Bitwise Operator

Meaning of operator "<<" is Shifts the bits to left

Meaning of operator ">>" is Shifts the bits to right

Meaning of operator "~" is Bitwise inversion (One's complement)

Meaning of operator "&" is Bitwise logical and

Meaning of operator "|" is Bitwise logical or

Meaning of operator "^" is Bitwise exclusive or

Assignment Operators

Operator sign "=" Example "m=10" Equivalent Expression "m=10"

Operator sign "+=" Example "m+=10" Equivalent Expression "m = m+10"

Operator sign "-=" Example "m-=10" Equivalent Expression "m = m-10"

Operator sign "*=" Example "m*=10" Equivalent Expression "m = m*10"

Operator sign "/=" Example "m/=10" Equivalent Expression "m = m/10"

Operator sign "%=" Example "m%=" Equivalent Expression "m = m%10"

Operator sign ">>=" Example "a>>=b" Equivalent Expression "a=a>>b"

Operator sign ">>>=" Example "a>>>=b" Equivalent Expression "a=a>>>b"

Operator sign "&=" Example "a&=b" Equivalent Expression "a=a&b"

Operator sign "^=" Example "a^=b" Equivalent Expression "a=a^b"

Operator sign "|=" Example "a|=b" Equivalent Expression "a=a|b"

Example : var m = 15; m += 10; document.write(m); Answer = 25.
